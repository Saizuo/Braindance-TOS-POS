Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12/21/2024

Welcome to Braindance (the "Bot"). We value your privacy and are committed to transparency about how your data is used. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we process, why it is necessary, and how users can manage their data.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Braindance, the following information may be processed:

  • Message Content: The content of messages sent in servers where the Bot is active is processed in real-time but not stored.
  • Server and User Data: Basic server configurations and settings required for the Bot's operation.
  • Level Tracking Data: User activity levels are tracked, but no message content is stored in the process.

2. Why Message Content Is Needed

Message content is processed to provide the following features:

  • Automod (Automatic Moderation):
    • Automod uses OpenAI's moderation technology to analyze messages for harmful, inappropriate, or spam content.
    • This feature can be enabled, disabled, or configured in the Bot's settings by server administrators.
  • Level Tracking:
    • User activity is tracked to assign experience points and levels. The Bot does not store message content for this feature.

3. How We Process Data

  • Real-Time Analysis: Message content is analyzed in real-time for moderation or level tracking purposes and is not stored.
  • Flagged Messages: If a message triggers the Automod feature, it is forwarded to the server's designated log channel for review by server administrators. The Bot does not store flagged messages beyond their transmission to the log channel.

4. Your Data Rights

We respect your rights and provide the following options:

  • Request Deletion: Users can contact the server administrators to request the removal of flagged messages from the log channel or request removal of their level data.
  • Opt-Out of Automod: Server owners or admins can disable the Automod feature through the Bot's settings, preventing message content analysis.

5. How to Contact Us

For inquiries or data-related concerns, please contact:

Support Server: https://discord.gg/ma4Gu6DxFK

6. Third-Party Services

Braindance uses OpenAI's moderation AI technology to process message content. Data analyzed by OpenAI is subject to their Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

  • The Bot does not retain or store any messages.
  • Flagged messages are sent directly to the log channel, where server administrators determine their retention duration.
  • Level tracking data (such as experience points) is stored securely without retaining any message content.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to server administrators through the Bot or other appropriate channels.

By using Braindance, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
